About the Algorithm

This colour palette generator uses colour theory algorithms to create harmonious colour schemes based on your selected base colour.

The algorithm converts your chosen colour from RGB to HSL (Hue, Saturation, Lightness) colour space, then calculates related colours based on different harmony rules:

  • Complementary Uses colours directly opposite on the colour wheel (180° hue difference). Creates high contrast and vibrant schemes.
  • Analogous Uses colours adjacent to each other on the colour wheel (30° hue difference). Creates serene and comfortable designs.
  • Triadic Uses three colours evenly spaced around the colour wheel (120° hue difference). Creates vibrant yet balanced schemes.
  • Monochromatic Uses variations in saturation and lightness of a single hue. Creates cohesive and elegant designs.

Each generated palette includes five colours: Primary (your selected colour), Secondary, Accent, Background, and Text. The algorithm automatically adjusts saturation and lightness to ensure good contrast and visual appeal.
